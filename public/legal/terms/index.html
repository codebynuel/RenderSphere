Acceptable Use

Use RenderSphere only for Blender scenes and assets you own or are allowed to process. Do not upload illegal, harmful, infringing, or abusive content.

Billing and Limits

Usage may be billed based on render time. Frame limits, upload limits, and concurrency limits can change as the service evolves.

Uploaded Files

RenderSphere stores uploaded `.blend` files and render outputs long enough to process and deliver jobs. Do not upload sensitive files unless you are comfortable using a cloud render service.

No Production Guarantee

Render jobs may fail because of scene issues, worker limits, GPU availability, third-party provider outages, or bugs. RenderSphere is provided without uptime or completion guarantees.
