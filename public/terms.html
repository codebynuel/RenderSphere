Terms of Use

RenderSphere is currently an MVP. The service may change, pause, or fail while it is being tested.

Acceptable Use

Use RenderSphere only for Blender scenes and assets you own or are allowed to process. Do not upload illegal, harmful, infringing, or abusive content.

Render Credits and Limits

Accounts may receive a small number of starter render credits. Credits, frame limits, upload limits, and concurrency limits can change while the MVP is being tested.

Uploaded Files

RenderSphere stores uploaded `.blend` files and render outputs long enough to process and deliver jobs. Do not upload sensitive files unless you are comfortable testing an MVP service.

No Production Guarantee

Render jobs may fail because of scene issues, worker limits, GPU availability, third-party provider outages, or bugs. RenderSphere is provided without uptime or completion guarantees during the MVP period.

Contact

Questions or takedown requests: support@rendersphere.app.
